About Nicolas

The Mind Behind
the Method

Nicolas Medicamento is an internationally recognised speaker, trainer and Neuroencoding Specialist who has worked with individuals and teams across 20+ countries — from 1-to-1 settings to audiences of over 500 people. His work spans entry-level professionals to C-suite executives, business owners and future leaders.

Over the past 10+ years, Nicolas has delivered transformational programmes across Malaysia, the Dominican Republic, Monaco, the Netherlands, Mexico, Germany, France, the UAE (Dubai & Abu Dhabi) and throughout the UK. His work blends psychology and neuroscience with real-world business strategy — everything he teaches is grounded in how people perform, communicate, and show up under pressure.

As a certified Neuroencoding Specialist, Nicolas helps leaders break through invisible mental barriers, communicate with extraordinary influence, and build organisations that operate at their highest potential.
